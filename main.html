Common Questions

"When you book Joyce Domestic, you get me. No contractors, no strangers—just my personal commitment to your home's perfection."

Avaliable upon booking request
01

Will anyone else accompany you?

Never. To ensure the highest level of security and consistent quality, I work entirely alone. You will always know exactly who is in your home, providing absolute peace of mind.

02

What is your privacy protocol?

My business is built on discretion. I operate under strict self-imposed NDAs and have extensive experience working within high-profile residences where privacy is the primary requirement.

03

How do you handle delicate materials?

I specialize in bespoke care. I personally select pH-neutral, museum-grade agents for rare stone, silk wall coverings, and artisan hardwoods to ensure longevity and preservation.

04

Are you insured for private estates?

Yes. As a professional sole proprietor, I maintain comprehensive liability insurance tailored for luxury residential environments and high-value contents.
